The Mission

Preserving Identity Through Art

The Essence project focuses on rural South African identity. We aim to empower local artists. We use visual storytelling to preserve culture. Our work aligns with National Arts Council 2026 goals. We prioritize youth employment plus social cohesion. We document traditional attire plus rural daily living. Our lens captures the earth along with the people who walk upon it. We believe in the power of the local image.
