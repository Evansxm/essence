FULL DAILY WORKSHOP SCHEDULE
This document details the hourly flow for every session of the four week program.
Time allocation includes translation to local languages plus slow pacing for clarity.

WEEK ONE SMARTPHONE PHOTOGRAPHY
Goal Mastering visual storytelling with mobile devices.

Day 1 Monday Light plus Shadows
09 00 to 10 00 Welcome Circle. Song of unity. Introduction to the Essence goal.
10 00 to 11 00 Theory. What is light. Identifying hard versus soft light sources.
11 00 to 12 00 Practical. The Light Walk. Learners find shadows under trees plus huts.
12 00 to 13 00 Lunch Break.
13 00 to 14 00 Theory. Introduction to reflectors using white paper.
14 00 to 15 00 Practical. Filling shadows on faces using the paper reflectors.
15 00 to 16 00 Review. Group sits in a circle to share one photo each.

Day 2 Tuesday The Art of the Portrait
09 00 to 10 00 Theory. Ethics of photography. Asking permission from elders.
10 00 to 11 00 Practical. The Eye Level game. Shooting from high plus low angles.
11 00 to 12 00 Practical. Window Portraits. Using a hut window as a studio light.
12 00 to 13 00 Lunch Break.
13 00 to 14 00 Theory. Backgrounds matter. How to clean the frame.
14 00 to 15 00 Practical. Environmental Portraits. Capturing a craftsman at work.
15 00 to 16 00 Review. Selecting the most honest portrait of the day.

Day 3 Wednesday Editing for Impact
09 00 to 10 00 Theory. Introduction to free mobile editing apps.
10 00 to 11 00 Practical. Cropping plus straightening horizons.
11 00 to 12 00 Practical. Adjusting brightness plus contrast.
12 00 to 13 00 Lunch Break.
13 00 to 14 00 Theory. Understanding filters plus mood. When to use black plus white.
14 00 to 15 00 Activity. Swap phones. Edit a photo taken by a partner.
15 00 to 16 00 Review. Before plus After showcase.

Day 4 Thursday Composition Rules
09 00 to 10 00 Theory. The Rule of Thirds. Enabling grid lines on phones.
10 00 to 11 00 Practical. Placing subjects on the intersection lines.
11 00 to 12 00 Practical. Leading Lines. Using fences plus roads to guide the eye.
12 00 to 13 00 Lunch Break.
13 00 to 14 00 Theory. Framing. Looking through windows or holes in walls.
14 00 to 15 00 Practical. Village Walk. Finding natural frames in nature.
15 00 to 16 00 Review. Critique session focusing only on composition.

Day 5 Friday Review plus Selection
09 00 to 10 00 Activity. Group curation. Reviewing all photos from the week.
10 00 to 11 00 Activity. Printing preparation. Selecting the top five images.
11 00 to 12 00 Theory. Digital hygiene. Backing up photos to the cloud or drive.
12 00 to 13 00 Lunch Break.
13 00 to 14 00 Activity. The Photo Essay. Arranging 3 photos to tell a short story.
14 00 to 15 00 Feedback. Facilitator gives individual feedback notes.
15 00 to 16 00 Closing. Weekly reflection circle plus song.

WEEK TWO PROFESSIONAL CAMERAS
Goal transitioning to DSLR technical mastery.

Day 1 Monday Buttons plus Dials
09 00 to 10 00 Safety. Strap discipline plus lens cap care.
10 00 to 11 00 Theory. The Camera Map. Drawing and labeling the body.
11 00 to 12 00 Practical. Holding the camera. Stance plus breathing.
12 00 to 13 00 Lunch Break.
13 00 to 14 00 Theory. Menu navigation. Formatting cards plus setting quality.
14 00 to 15 00 Practical. The Focus Drill. Switching between auto plus manual focus.
15 00 to 16 00 Review. Quiz on camera parts.

Day 2 Tuesday Exposure Triangle
09 00 to 10 00 Theory. Understanding Shutter Speed. Freezing time versus blur.
10 00 to 11 00 Practical. Jumping shots. Shooting at high shutter speeds.
11 00 to 12 00 Practical. Panning. following a running child to blur the background.
12 00 to 13 00 Lunch Break.
13 00 to 14 00 Theory. Aperture. Understanding f stops plus light intake.
14 00 to 15 00 Practical. Depth of Field. Blurring the background for portraits.
15 00 to 16 00 Review. Examining metadata to see settings.

Day 3 Wednesday ISO plus Lenses
09 00 to 10 00 Theory. ISO sensitivity. Grain versus clean images.
10 00 to 11 00 Practical. Shooting inside a dark room versus outside in sun.
11 00 to 12 00 Theory. Lenses. Wide angle versus Telephoto zoom.
12 00 to 13 00 Lunch Break.
13 00 to 14 00 Practical. Perspective. How a wide lens distorts a face.
14 00 to 15 00 Activity. The Zoom Challenge. Standing in one spot plus changing focal length.
15 00 to 16 00 Review. Discussing which lens felt best for portraits.

Day 4 Thursday Advanced Control
09 00 to 10 00 Theory. White Balance. Tungsten versus Daylight settings.
10 00 to 11 00 Practical. Correcting orange skin tones indoors.
11 00 to 12 00 Theory. Metering Modes. Spot versus Matrix metering.
12 00 to 13 00 Lunch Break.
13 00 to 14 00 Practical. Backlit subjects. Exposing for the face not the sun.
14 00 to 15 00 Activity. Silhouette shooting. Making the subject black against the sky.
15 00 to 16 00 Review. analyzing exposure histograms.

Day 5 Friday Practical Application
09 00 to 10 00 Briefing. Assignment details for the market shoot.
10 00 to 11 00 Field Trip. Visit to the local market or gathering place.
11 00 to 12 00 Field Trip. Shooting a mini documentary series of a vendor.
12 00 to 13 00 Lunch Break.
13 00 to 14 00 Lab. Transferring images to the computer.
14 00 to 15 00 Selection. Choosing the best 10 images from the market.
15 00 to 16 00 Closing. Weekly wrap up plus equipment check.

WEEK THREE VIDEOGRAPHY
Goal Moving images plus sound design.

Day 1 Monday Visual Narrative
09 00 to 10 00 Theory. What makes a movie. Beginning plus Middle plus End.
10 00 to 11 00 Activity. Story Circle. Telling local legends.
11 00 to 12 00 Activity. Storyboarding. Drawing frames on paper.
12 00 to 13 00 Lunch Break.
13 00 to 14 00 Theory. Shot Types. Wide plus Medium plus Close Up.
14 00 to 15 00 Practical. Filming the shot list. No audio yet.
15 00 to 16 00 Review. Watching raw clips to check framing.

Day 2 Tuesday Sound plus Audio
09 00 to 10 00 Theory. The importance of sound. Bad sound kills a movie.
10 00 to 11 00 Practical. Recording ambience. Birds plus wind plus traffic.
11 00 to 12 00 Practical. The Interview. Mic placement for clear voice.
12 00 to 13 00 Lunch Break.
13 00 to 14 00 Activity. The Silent Movie. Filming a scene where sound is added later.
14 00 to 15 00 Practical. Foley Art. Making sound effects with hands plus feet.
15 00 to 16 00 Review. Listening to recordings with eyes closed.

Day 3 Wednesday Camera Movement
09 00 to 10 00 Theory. Motivation for movement. Why do we move the camera.
10 00 to 11 00 Practical. The Tripod Pan. Smooth horizon scanning.
11 00 to 12 00 Practical. The Tilt. Revealing a subject from feet to head.
12 00 to 13 00 Lunch Break.
13 00 to 14 00 Theory. Handheld stability. The T Rex walk.
14 00 to 15 00 Practical. Tracking shot. Following a walking subject.
15 00 to 16 00 Review. Checking footage for unwanted shake.

Day 4 Thursday Editing Basics
09 00 to 10 00 Theory. The Timeline. How editing software works.
10 00 to 11 00 Practical. Importing footage plus organizing bins.
11 00 to 12 00 Practical. The Rough Cut. Assembling the story in order.
12 00 to 13 00 Lunch Break.
13 00 to 14 00 Practical. Trimming. Making cuts tight plus rhythmic.
14 00 to 15 00 Practical. Adding Music. Balancing voice levels with background tracks.
15 00 to 16 00 Review. Screening rough cuts for feedback.

Day 5 Friday Final Production
09 00 to 10 00 Lab. Final polish. Adding titles plus credits.
10 00 to 11 00 Lab. Color grading. Giving the film a look.
11 00 to 12 00 Lab. Exporting. Saving the file for playback.
12 00 to 13 00 Lunch Break.
13 00 to 14 00 Screening. The Village Film Festival. Watching all films.
14 00 to 15 00 Feedback. Voting for the best story.
15 00 to 16 00 Closing. Weekly reflection plus celebration.
